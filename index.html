Chocolate Cupcake

I asked ChatGPT: "how do you make chocolate cupcakes?"

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (180째C). Line a 12-count muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate large mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, until just combined.
  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared muffin cups.
  6. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  7. Remove the cupcakes from the oven and let them cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the cupcakes are cooled, you can frost them with your favorite frosting.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cupcakes!

Strawberry Cupcake

I asked ChatGPT: "how do you make strawberry cupcakes?"

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1 cup (150g) chopped fresh strawberries
